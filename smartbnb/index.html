Democratizing technology for short-term rental hosts.

I joined smartbnb - an automation tool for short-term rental industry - in late 2018 as a product designer to lead the visual direction of the new web app that'll be heavily used by short-term rental hosts worldwide.

After spending 6 months improving the current version and learning more about the userbase, I dived deep into building a new, more consistent and sustainable design which allowed us not only fix the known usability problems but also leaving enough wiggle room for new features to come.

I also took on the new branding and marketing website since we faiiled to find a good outsource team for these projects.

The cool kid of the industry.

After nine months of sweat, tears, countless amount of coffee and meetings, we launched smartbnb v.2 in autumn 2019.

The hard work we've put in the complete overhaul of a platform that is used daily by hundreds of hosts has paid off not only in appraisal but also a significant jump in the MRR and NPS scores.

Working as a one-man-design-team in this project was both satisfying in terms of outcome but also one of the highlights of my life. 10/10 would do it again!
